24 Best Business Intelligence(BI) Tools List in 2020

Details
Last Updated: 20 March 2020

What is Business Intelligence Tool?

BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE (BI) tools are software that collects, transforms, presents data to help decision-makers drive business growth. BI tools ingest large amounts of structured and unstructured data from varied sources, transform it and help deduce actionable business insights from the data.

Here are the Top BI tools with its popular features and download links. The list contains both open source(free) and commercial(paid) software.

1) Grow

Grow is a cloud business intelligence platform that allows all users, whether experienced data analysts or business users, to take control of their data and find actionable insights

Features

  • 115+ ready-made data connectors for commonly used systems (Salesforce, Bronto, Marketo).
  • 20 different data visualizations for creating custom dashboards and telling your data story.
  • Unlimited user accounts.
  • Simple user interface for managing SQL and ready-made transforms.
  • Grow's app, Slack integration, scheduled email sends, and permalinks make sharing insights with the rest of your team simple.
  • Hosted on the Grow cloud, supported constantly by Grow's dedicated team of developers.

2) Datapine

Datapine is a powerful and easy-to-use BI software, that enables data analysts and nontechnical users alike to easily visualize, analyze & explore growing data from multiple data sources

Features:

  • Fast & easy data connectors to integrate all common data sources in seconds
  • An intuitive drag & drop user interface to build stunning data visualizations with a few clicks
  • Advanced BI dashboards with modern, interactive dashboard features
  • Over 80 predefined dashboard templates for different industries and departments
  • Multiple reporting options including live dashboards, automated email reports, secure and customized viewer areas as well as embedded dashboards

Free Trial link: https://www.datapine.com/registration/bi/

Zoho Analytics is a self-service business intelligence and analytics platform. It allows users to create insightful dashboards and visually analyze any data in minutes. It features an AI powered assistant that enables users to ask questions and get intelligent answers in the form of meaningful reports.

Features:

  • 100+ readymade connectors for popular business apps, cloud drives and databases.
  • Wide variety of visualization options--charts, pivot tables, summary views, KPI widgets and custom themed dashboards.
  • Unified business analytics for analyzing data from across business apps.
  • Augmented analytics using AI, ML and NLP.
  • White label BI portals and embedded analytics solutions.
