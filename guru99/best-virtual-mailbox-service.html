13 BEST Virtual Mailbox for Business and Travelers

Details
Last Updated: 28 February 2020

Virtual Mailbox is a digital mailbox service which you can access by using a PC or mobile. This service can be used by frequent travelers, startups, and small businesses to check mail without physically checking their mailbox.

Following is a handpicked list of Cheapest Virtual Mailbox Service, with their popular features and website links.

1) Earthclassmail

Earthclassmail is a virtual mailbox service provider that upload mail and paper to cloud storage devices such as Dropbox, Google Drive, and Box. It allows you to integrate checks into accounting software like Xero and QuickBooks Online.

Features:

  • It has a scanning feature that converts paper documents into digital documents.
  • You can route postal mail to Earthclassmail using a virtual address.
  • It allows you to view, search, tag, manage, download, and integration with the cloud.
  • This service provider helps you to scan and deposit checks.
  • You can use UPS shipping labels to send paper.
  • It produces high-Resolution PDF.
  • Supported cities: Albuquerque, Anchorage, Atlanta, Boulder, Baltimore, Billings, and 50+ other locations.

Link: https://www.earthclassmail.com/

2) Virtualpostmail

VirtualPostMail offers services to manage online mail, documents as well as storage. It enables you to read scanned postal mail online anytime, anywhere.

Features:

  • It provides a permanent mailbox address.
  • You can directly receive your deposit check into VirtualPostMail account.
  • It provides envelope forwarding options to anywhere in the world.
  • You can combine virtual mailbox with VirtualPostMail for business mail solutions.
  • Supported cities: Delaware, Henderson, California, Walnut, Nevada.

Link: https://www.virtualpostmail.com/

Anytime Mailbox gives mail center operators and business centers the ability to offer their customers a digital mailbox in a few minutes. You can preview your digital address for each location you have selected.

  • You can view and manage mail on any device.
