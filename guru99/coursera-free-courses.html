FAQ

1. How much does Coursera courses cost?

Coursera is available either on a subscription basis or for individual purchase. Many of Specializations course costing between US $40-80 per month. Many, of courses, allows you to take benefit of 7-day free trial; after that, you will be billed.

2. How will taking Coursera courses help you achieve your career goals?

Here, are some common benefits you will get if you enrolled for Coursera courses.

  • Coursera courses help you to learn advanced topics and enhance your technical knowledge.
  • Helps you to upgrade your self with the latest changes about the course you have taken.
  • Global recognization of Coursera courses helps you get better packages in your career.

3. What happens if I fail a Coursera course?

Most courses included in Coursera have personalized deadlines that begin when you enroll in a course. There is no penalty imposed for missing your deadline. This allows you to take your own time in case if you don’t want to work to meet the suggested deadlines.

4. How to unenroll from Coursera course?

Here, is a process to unenroll from specific Coursera course:

  • Log with your Coursera account.
  • You need to open the list of courses which you have enrolled
  • For that click on Enrollments option on Coursera’s home page.
  • Click on unenroll option next to the course; you will see three-dot
  • Click on them to open the menu.
  • Select Leave Course option.

5. How to enroll in a Coursera course for free?

Many courses available in Coursera have an audit option, which allows you to to access course materials for free.

Here, are steps to Enroll for a specific course for free:

  • Open the home page of the course which you wants to Enroll
  • Click on the Enroll for free option
  • Fill the given subscription form.
  • In the next screen, click the Start Free Trial button.
  • Enter the details of your Credit card or Paypal details.
  • Start Free Trial button at the bottom of the screen. You will get 1-week free trial for that course.
