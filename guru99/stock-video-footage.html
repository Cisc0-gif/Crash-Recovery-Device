17 BEST Stock Videos Sites in 2020 [Paid/Royalty Free Footage]

Details
Last Updated: 18 March 2020

Stock Videos are short length clips which can be used for film making and personal purpose. Finding the right video clips is very tricky. There are many stock video sites which help you to download high-quality video clips with no royalties. Once you download them, you can use them as many times as you like.

Following is a handpicked list of Top Paid Stock Videos with their popular features and website links.

1) Shutterstock

Shutterstock is a company that provides high-quality licensed videos. You can buy and use it for your personal and business projects. This online platform offers numerous videos in 4K and HD formats.

Feature:

  • You can search clips by resolution, frames per second, length, etc.
  • It provides a wide range of video categories like buildings, Wildlife, Art, and more.
  • This site automatically adds fresh videos every week.
  • You can download videos captured using the latest technology and camera.
  • The video combines artistic and technical excellence.

Link: https://www.shutterstock.com/royalty-free/video-categories

2) Videvo

Videvo offers free stock video footage that can be used in any project. You can download video clips in 4K and HD formats. It allows you to use videos as many times you like.

Feature:

  • You can search more than thousands of stock videos.
  • It has a hassle-free licensing.
  • Videvo uploads videos daily.
  • It helps you to download videos according to shot types like framing, angle, and focal length.

Link: https://www.videvo.net

3) videezy

Videezy enables you to browse and download a vast range of videos. You can get HD stock background clips and video footage.

Feature:

  • Videezy offers category wise easy to find videos.
  • You can sort videos by the best match or newest.
  • This site provides numerous backgrounds that can be enhanced for further use.
  • You can utilize SD, HD -720, HD -1080, and 4K resolution videos.
  • It enables you to download videos in MP4 and MOV file formats.

Link: https://www.videezy.com

4) Flickr

Flickr is a video hosting company that enables you to share clips with others. It helps you to store, sort, and search videos online.

Feature:

  • This website provides various categories to download videos.
  • It allows you to find videos according to camera types.
  • You can sort out videos by name, activity, oldest, newest, etc.

Link: https://www.flickr.com/search

5) Morguefile

MorgueFile is a website database for downloading high-quality videos for either corporate or public use. It enables you to modify, resize, and customize the videos.

Feature:

  • You can create a board (collection) to save your videos online.
  • Morguefile offers royalty-free stock video clips.
  • It allows you to download a 4K video.
  • This company offers a wide range of footage for making a film.
  • You can search and download office videos, people video, occupation videos, and more.
  • It enables you to download videos from your mobile phone.

Link: https://morguefile.com/videos

6) Bigstockphoto

Bigstockphoto is a website that contains a wide variety of videos for commercial use. It allows you to choose license types according to the model released for editorial or non-editorial use.

Feature:

  • You can search the most popular, relevant, or new videos.
  • The result can be filtered by resolution and keyword.
  • It provides support via live chat, phone, or email.

Link: https://www.bigstockphoto.com/video/search/

7) Videoblocks

Videoblocks is the site that allows you to download and use videos for individual or business purposes. Once you login into account of this website, you will get varieties of royalty-free clips.

Feature:

  • The library of Videoblocks contains millions of 4K and HD videos.
  • You can download as many stock videos clips as you need.
  • Videoblocks helps you to find news segment and text animation for YouTube.
  • It enables you to download stock footage, motion backgrounds, and after effect templates.

Link: https://www.videoblocks.com

8) Motionelements

Motionelements provides a place to get videos for commercial use. It offers a fully localized experience for stock media buyers and sellers across the world.

Feature:

  • You do not require to pay additional licensing fees
  • It enables you to use clips in unlimited projects forever.
  • This site is safe for commercial use.
  • You can utilize videos for any project type, including the advertisement, events, blogs, websites, YouTube, etc.
  • It supports payment methods like a credit card, bank transfer, or PayPal.

Link: https://www.motionelements.com/free/stock-footage

9) Dissolve

Dissolve is a platform that allows you to download footage for various purposes. It helps you to explore various shoots and themes.

Feature:

  • You can download footage in SD, HD, and 4K file formats.
  • Videos are shot by professional filmmakers for commercial projects.
  • It provides categorywise videos like fine art, fashion, and film.
  • You can explore clips based on human poses.

Link: https://dissolve.com

10) Filmsupply

Filmsupply offers filmmakers, producers, and agencies footage. It provides video clips from many filmmakers. You can send a brief concept or ideas to filmsupply, and it will search for the clip.

Feature:

  • It enables you to download videos for commercial and editorial use.
  • You can filter out videos according to filmmakers, moods, people, and more.
  • This platform provides numerous themes like winter, summer, joy, love, etc.

Link: https://www.filmsupply.com

11) RocketStock

RocketStock is a provider of After Effects templates and assets. It can be used by motion designers, video editors, filmmakers, and cinematographers.

Feature:

  • RocketStock offers a wide range of video element packs.
  • It has a collection of After Effects templates.
  • You can use your pack in as many projects as you like.
  • It has a built-in video customization tool.
  • This website provides detailed video tutorials.

Link: https://www.rocketstock.com

12) 123RF

123RF is a stock content agency. It has a collection of videos that are checked for legal and model issues. The website of this company supports English, Dutch, French, and more languages.

Feature:

  • You can find videos in resolutions like 240WD, 480SD, 720HD, 4K, etc.
  • You can get clips related to beauty, education, animals, abstract, lifestyle, etc.
  • It uploads more than 90,000 content on daily basis.
  • You can customize a plan for your team or business.

Link: https://www.123rf.com/stock-footage/

13) Pond5

Pond5 is a New York-based website for downloading royalty-free videos. It has a large collection of stock footage. You can share downloaded files with others.

Feature:

  • It provides more than 17 million stock video clips.
  • Thousands of videos are added daily.
  • You can upload your own video to see similar footage.
  • Uploaded videos can be filtered by composition or color.
  • Pond5 helps you to find videos, TV series, and more.
  • It provides integration for all major video editing software.
  • This organization provides footages for celebration, lifestyle, Animal, business, and more.

Link: https://www.pond5.com/stock-video-footage/

14) Storyandheart

Storyandheart is a collaborative filmmaking and story-driven video licensing platform. It provides a place to download stories with videos.

Feature:

  • You can browse stories to search your clips.
  • It allows you to select human moods to browse videos.
  • Storyandheart helps you to connect your audience with genuine footage.
  • Uploaded videos are made by a worldwide community of filmmakers.

Link: https://www.storyandheart.com

15) IStock

IStock provides an online platform to explore exclusive royalty-free stock videos. It helps you to discover 4K, HD (High Definition), and SD (Standard Definition) video.

Feature:

  • It provides video categories like nature, architecture, healthcare, food, drink, etc.
  • You can use these videos virtually for any project as many times as you want.
  • Istock enables you to sort videos by best match, newest, and most popular.
  • You can search for videos by upload date and duration.

Link: https://www.istockphoto.com/in/footage

16) Clipstill

Clipstill is a platform that enables you to utilize videos for marketing and web usage. When you download videos, Clipstill will allow you to use them for any charity or educational purpose.

Feature:

  • You can get videos in MPEG4 (Moving Picture Experts Group) and GIF (Graphics Interchange Format) format.
  • It enables you to download clips based on people, places, food, nature, etc.
  • You can receive an email when a new item is uploaded to Clipstill.

Link: https://www.clipstill.com

17) Adobe Stock

Adobe stock provides royalty-free videos in HD and 4K resolution. It provides motion graphics templates and video loops. You can use videos as many times as you like times after downloading them.

Feature:

  • It adds videos on a daily basis.
  • This app can be used across multiple devices.
  • You can easily find videos with aesthetic search filters.
  • One plan can be used by unlimited users.
  • Adobe Stock enables you to access media files anytime, anywhere.
  • It has simplified the billing process.

Link: https://stock.adobe.com/video
