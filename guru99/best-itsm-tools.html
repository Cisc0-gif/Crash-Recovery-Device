29 BEST ITSM Tools in 2020

Details
Last Updated: 03 February 2020

IT Service Management, which is popularly known (ITSM) aims to align the delivery of information technology services with the needs of the enterprise. The focus of ITSM tools is to deliver satisfactory service to the end user.

There are many ITSM tools available in the market.

Following is a curated list of Top 29 handpicked ITSM software with popular features and latest download links. This comparison list contains open source as well as commercial tools.

1) ServiceDesk Plus

ServiceDesk Plus is the ITIL-ready service desk software with integrated asset and project management capabilities. With advanced ITSM functionality and easy-to-use capabilities, this ITSM tool helps IT support teams deliver world-class service to end users with reduced costs and complexity. Available on-premise and on cloud, ServiceDesk Plus comes in three different editions and is available in 29 different languages.

Features:

  • A flexible and easy-to-use interface, resulting in a short learning curve
  • Leverages the latest in technology, including virtual assistants and AI capabilities
  • Tight, contextual integrations with other IT management and business apps
  • Highly customizable and scalable for different process maturities
  • Best-practice process workflows and features available out-of-the-box

2) FreshService

FreshService is an ITIL-ready cloud service desk platform. The tool is capable of offering a range of ITSM support at any scale to businesses of any type or size. The tool is available for free for a 21-day trial.

Features:

  • Availability of mobile apps
  • Features for root cause analysis of incidents
  • Problem, Project and Configuration management
  • Offers Self Service Portal

3) ServiceNow

ServiceNow IT Service Management tool. This ITSM tool helps you to aggregate IT Services into a single cloud-based platform. It offers easy Interaction with legacy apps.

Features:
