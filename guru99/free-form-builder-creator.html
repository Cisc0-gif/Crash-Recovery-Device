21 BEST Online Form Builders (Creators) in 2020 [Free/Paid]

Details
Last Updated: 28 February 2020

Form Builders/Creators are cloud applications that help you to develop highly customizable and job-specific forms. They can be used to collect user responses in real time, and store essential information via quiz, collect payments and more.

Following is a handpicked list of Top Form Creators, with their popular features and website links. The list contains both open source(free) and commercial(paid) software.

1) forms.app

forms.app is an easy to use online web form creation tool established a couple of years ago. It offers quite a number of templates, making everything easier for you. You do not have to register to start using it.

Prominent Features:

  • Start without registration
  • Easy use
  • The free package includes all features and adequate capacity
  • Customizable design and beautiful themes
  • Payment collection
  • E-Shopping system with product basket tool ready in minutes
  • GPS Location
  • Integrate to 1500+ apps with Zapier
  • Track conversions via Google Ads and Facebook Pixel
  • Google Analytics Integration

2) Leadformly

Leadformly is an online application that enables users to create lead capture forms. It can be integrated with more than 1,000 tools.

Features:

  • You can assign a value to leads based on their answers, making it easy to see which leads are useful for you.
  • It allows you to send leads directly from your LeadForms into your marketing automation, CRM, and other tools.
  • This tool blocks spammers.
  • You can segment your leads based on the criteria.
  • Leadformly automatically detects visitor location and prefills information like country and phone number extension.

Link: https://leadformly.com/

Jotform is an application that helps you to design professional looking forms. It enables you to customize with advanced styling options to match your branding.

Features:

  • You can save time and effort by automating a task.

  • Helps you to shortlist interview candidates
  • You will receive an email for responses you got.

Link: https://www.jotform.com/

  • You can easily manage event registration or collect emails for a newsletter.

Link: https://www.google.com/forms/about/

Features:
