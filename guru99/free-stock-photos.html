30 Best FREE Stock Photos Sites in 2020

Details
Last Updated: 18 March 2020

Stock images sites are a great resource to get high-quality HD photos and image which you can use in your designs for various purposes. These images are an ideal source for marketing, advertising, personal or commercial use. It also used for creative projects, publishing websites and blogs, etc.

Following is a handpicked list of Top Stock Photo sites, with their popular features and website links. The list contains both open source(free) and commercial(paid) software.

1) Pexels

Pexels is a free image hosting tool that offers high quality and free stock photos licensed under the Pexels license. It allows you to put your photos in front of millions of visitors and promote your website, blog, or social media profile.

Features:

  • Help millions of people to find photos or images for their website.
  • Offers high-quality free stock photos
  • Easily create and manage your portfolio
  • It helps millions of creating to develop beautiful products and designs.

Link: https://www.pexels.com/
