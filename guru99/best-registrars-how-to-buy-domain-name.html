FAQ

What is a Domain Name?

A domain name is an address where Internet users able to access their website.

Why do you need a Domain Name?

Your registered domain name helps you to protect copyrights and trademarks, increase brand awareness, and improves search engine positioning of your website.

Can you change a Domain name once you register it?

Yes, you can change the domain name of your website.

What is a TLD?

TLD refers to the Top-level domain (TLD) refers to the last segment of a domain name or the part which follows immediately after "dot" symbol. TLDs are classified into two categories: generic TLDs and country-specific TLDs.

What does registrar suspension mean?

It means a registrar's ability to sponsor new domain names or initiate inbound transfers is temporarily suspended by your domain name registration website.

Who should register a domain name?

Individual or a firm who wants to create his or her presence on the Internet should register a domain name.

My site is not ready, can I still register my domain name?

Yes, you can and use it when your site is ready.
