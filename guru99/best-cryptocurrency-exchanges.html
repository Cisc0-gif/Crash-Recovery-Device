14 BEST Cryptocurrency(Bitcoin) Exchanges in 2020

Details
Last Updated: 09 March 2020

A cryptocurrency exchange, also called a Digital Currency Exchange (DCU) is a business that enables you to trade digital currencies or cryptocurrencies. Many cryptocurrency exchanges offer to trade Bitcoins, Ethereum, XRP (Ripple), Altcoin, and more.

Following are Top Cryptocurrency Exchanges with their popular features and website links. The list contains both open source(free) and commercial(paid) software.

1) N.Exchange

N.Exchange allows trading fiat to crypto and crypto to crypto easily, transparently and securely with little to no counterparty risk and a known-in-advance price for no additional cost.

Features:

  • 24 hours support via chat, zendesk and phone
  • Buy staking coins (Neo, Tezos, MakerDAO) directly with your credit card
  • Support all major stable coins (USDC, USDT, USDT Erc20, Maker)
  • Easy fiat on-ramp and off-ramp (Buy and sell Bitcoin and crypto instantly)
  • No daily and monthly limits (Fiat and Crypto)
  • Free fixed price, for 15 minutes to allow you a hassle-free buying experience
  • Lowest spreads on the market (amongst instant exchanges)
  • 30% Discount on credit card fees for VIP customers (pay as little as 4% credit card fee)

2) Binance

Binance is a crypto exchange that offers a platform for trading more than 150 cryptocurrencies. This online program has an API to integrate your current trading application.

Features:

  • This application offers a wide range of tools for trading online.
  • It provides 24/7 support.
  • Binance is compatible with Web, iOS, Android, and PC client.
  • It offers both basic and advanced exchange interfaces for trading.
  • It does 1.2 bn average daily trading volume with more than 1,400,000 transactions per second.

Link: https://www.binance.com/en

3) Coinbase

Coinbase is an online platform that can be utilized for purchasing, selling, transferring, as well as storing digital currency. It securely stores a wide range of digital assets in offline storage. This platform supports more than 100 countries.

Features:

  • You can buy and sell any digital currency and keep track of them in one place.
  • It provides app for both iOS and Android devices.
  • You can schedule your currency trading on daily, weekly, or monthly bases.
  • It stores your funds in a vault for safety purposes.
  • It is one of the largest cryptocurrency exchanges

Link: https://www.coinbase.com/

4) BitFlyer

BitFlyer is a cryptocurrency exchange that helps you to buy, sell Bitcoin and other digital currencies by paying nominal fees or payment. This cloud-based program uses the latest encryption to secure your Bitcoins.

Features:

  • It allows you to build your crypto portfolio in a secure way.
  • You can create custom alerts to get instant price updates.
  • This program works for both Android as well as iOS devices.
  • It enables you to buy crypto with USD(US Dollar).
  • You can execute complex trades through the REST API or the Lightning interface.

Link: https://bitflyer.com/en-eu/

5) KuCoin

KuCoin is a cryptocurrency exchange that provides the latest and secure platform to trade between cryptocurrencies. It provides a 24/7customer service.

Features:

  • It helps you to purchase Bitcoin safely.
  • This application offers you a quick and easy way of trading.
  • You can create KuCoin account in less time.

Link: https://www.kucoin.com/

6) Poloniex

Poloniex is an online platform for cryptocurrency exchange. You can use it to buy, sell, and trade 60+ crypto assets. This platform helps you to access airdrops (distribution of a cryptocurrency token or coin) and new projects.

Features:

  • You can connect this app to cards and banks.
  • It helps you to trade and store cryptocurrency like ETH, BTC, etc.
  • You can trade on margin across a range of pairs to get bigger returns.
  • You can run smart algorithms and bot trading strategies for a better result.

Link: https://poloniex.com/

7) Bitstamp

Bitstamp is a cloud-based application for crypto exchange. It offers analytical tools for experienced or new traders. This platform helps you to purchase cryptocurrency using numerous money deposit options, including credit cards.

Features:

  • It has no withdrawal limit.
  • This program allows you to connect WebSocket communication protocol for real time data streaming.
  • It keeps your assets in cold storage for security.
  • You can withdraw funds directly from your bank account.
  • It encrypts all personal data.
  • Bitstamp gives support via email or phone.

Link: https://www.bitstamp.net/

8) Cex.io

Cex.io is a platform that helps you to buy and sell Bitcoins. It allows you to deposit funds using MasterCard, Visa card, or PayPal Debit MasterCard. This online program follows scalping and frequency trading strategies to secure assets and data.

Features:

  • Users can trade USD for Bitcoins, Ethereum (open-source distributed computing platform), and XRP (Ripple).
  • It provides protection against DDOS(Distributed Denial-of-Service) attacks using full data encryption.
  • You can trade with more than 10x leverage without creating an extra account.
  • It supports platforms like mobile devices and websites.
  • Cex.io gives service in more than 35 states of the USA.
  • This application offers downloadable reports showing real time balance and transaction history.

Link: https://cex.io/

9) BitMex

BitMEX is a trading and cryptocurrency exchange platform. It is operated by HDR Global. This web-based application offers comprehensive API.

Features:

  • It helps investors to access financial markets using Bitcoin.
  • This application constantly audits the balance and history of all accounts.
  • It provides multi-factor security, inside and out.
  • Bitmex keeps all funds in cold storage for security.
  • It offers a variety of contract types.

Link: https://www.bitmex.com/

10) Changelly

Changelly is a cryptocurrency exchange that enables you to exchange crypto fast and buy it with a bank card. It provides a facility to trade BTC (Bitcoin), XRP, ETH, and LTC(Litecoin).

Features:

  • You can easily see the best rates currently available in the market.
  • You don't require any verification for exchanging transaction.
  • It supports more than 150 cryptocurrencies.
  • This program provides API to take advantage of more features.

Link: https://changelly.com/

11) Coinmama

Coinmama is a cryptocurrency exchange that allows you to buy cryptocurrencies with your credit card or debit card. This tool provides instant delivery of your coins.

Features:

  • You can buy the coins in the limit provided by this application.
  • It provides fast verification of your account.
  • You can buy ripple, Ethereum, Bitcoin, and other digital currencies.
  • Coinmama offers 24/7 hour support.

Link: https://www.coinmama.com

12) Coinsbit

Coinsbit is a trading platform that offers a safe crypto exchange solution. It helps you to exchange any digital currencies quickly. It provides a secure platform with OWASP (Open Web Application Security Project) standard.

Features:

  • It has processing speed up to 10,000 trades every second.
  • This application stores in cold wallets.
  • It uses WAF (Web Application Firewall) that detects and blocks hacker attacks.
  • It supports BTC, LTC (Litecoin), BCH (Bitcoin Cash), BTG (Bitcoin Gold), etc.

Link: https://coinsbit.io/

13) Luno

Luno is a cryptocurrency exchange platform that offers numerous APIs for trading automation. This application is secure and easy to buy and store cryptocurrencies.

Features:

  • It provides real time market details.
  • This tool offers charts for reading analytics.
  • Luno offers API for automating trading.
  • You can export a CSV (Comma-separated values) file.
  • This tool has a depth liquidity indicators.
  • You can deposit funds directly in a bank.

Link: https://www.luno.com/en/exchange

14) Bitrue

Bitrue is an online application for buying, selling, transferring, and storing digital currencies. It offers exchanges of Bitcoin Cash, Ethereum, Litecoin, and USD currencies worldwide. This application is easy to use and a safe platform for cryptocurrency trading.

Features:

  • It provides a global crypto trading platform.
  • Bitrue offers a multi-threaded, high-performance order matching engine.
  • It has a DDOS protection system for security.
  • You can access this platform on any device, including mobile devices and desktop.

Link: https://www.bitrue.com/

15) Coinall

CoinAll is an exchange which is owned by the OKEx company. It offers digital asset trading services of Litecoin, Ethereum, and Bitcoin to global traders.

Features:

  • It monitors listed projects to hide important data.
  • You can access this digital asset trading platform via software, or API.
  • This application does not disclose any personal information to third parties.
  • It allows you to scan QR code to download the application on the iOS phone.

Link: https://www.coinall.com/
