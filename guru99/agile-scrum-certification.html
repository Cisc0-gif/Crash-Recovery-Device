Best Agile and Scrum Certifications in 2020

Details
Last Updated: 18 March 2020

To cope up with rapidly changing IT market, and its growing demands and expectations, organizations have taken up agile methods and principles.

Agile is the need of the hour as demand for agile professionals has increased in the IT industry and picking up in other areas of engineering as well. This demand triggers the need for Agile certifications.

Agile certification helps you to accelerated software delivery, improve delivery predictability. It helps you to reduce the overall cost of a project. Moreover, this certification course also gives you global recognition and make you stand out of the crowd.

In this Agile certification tutorial, you will learn:

  • Who Can Take Up This Training?
  • Agile Certified Practitioner
  • ICAgile- Agile Certified Coach
  • SAFe® 4 Agilest
  • Certified Scrum Master
  • Certified Scrum Professional
  • Benefits of Agile Certification training

Who can Take Up This Training?

An agile certification training is recommended for the following professionals:

  • Working and aspiring agile coaches
  • Professionals who require to play a leadership role in Agile projects
  • Product Owners
  • Scrum Masters
  • Iteration Managers
  • Project Managers and Team Leaders

Agile Certified Practitioner

The Agile Certified Practitioner or ACP from the Project Management Institute (PMI) are project management professionals whose companies are either using or moving to agile practices.

It assures that the certification holder has real-world Experience for managing agile projects. The person also becomes familiar with many subsets of the agile methodology, including Kanban, Scrum, and Lean.

Certification Name PMI Agile Certficat Practitioner
Accreditation Body Agile Project Manager Institute
Prerequisites 2.000 hours of project experience which is working on teams.
Moreover, apart from the above stated hours, either 1500 hours of working on agile project teams or working with agile methodologies are required.
Career Path
  • PMP Scrum Master
  • Agile Trainer
  • Business Analyst
  • Technical Business Analyst
  • Project Manager
  • Agile Project Manager
  • Digital Channel planner
Cost of Certification
  • For PMI Members: US$435.00
  • For non-PMI members: US$495.00
Average Salary $123,000
Cost of Renewal The student can earn a maximum of 30 PDUs (professional development unit) in various course topics, every three years.

The exam details:

  • Duration of the Exam: 3 hours
  • Total Number of questions: 120 (Only multiple Choice)
  • Pass Percentage: There is no percentage mentioned in their site, but score above 70% is eligible for passing this exam.

ICAgile- Agile Certified Coach

This certification can be ideal for you if you want to be efficient, Agile Coach in a business environment. It works wonder for your career. It also helps you to gain proficient coaching skills by providing you with the aptitude to distinguish between training, mentoring, and coaching.

To get this Agile certificate, you need to attend the program for three days, and then you can get the certificate without giving any examination. As their focus is plentiful usage of discussions, completed case studies, study material with classroom exercises.

Certification ICP- ACC Training
Accreditation Body ICAgile
Prerequisites A basic idea of Agile Methodology and also small working experience as an Agile practitioner is recommended
Career Path Agile Coch
Average Salary $119.883
Cost of Certification $1795

Exam details:

  • Duration of the Exam: No exam
  • Total Number of questions: Not Applicable
  • Pass Percentage: NA
  • Requirements: Persson need to attend 16-hour training taught by an IC Agile authorized Training Partner

SAFe® 4 Agilest

SAFe Scaled Agilist, also known as SAFe or Scaled Agilist. It is a two days training course provided by the training providers. In this Agile course, the candidate able to learn Agile leadership principles and how to launch SAFe in agile.

The application who wants to learn transformational skills should join this course and get ahead in their careers as a job as a SAFe Agilist job.

Certification Leading SAFe Training course
Accreditation Body Scaled Agile
Prerequisites You need a minimum of 5 years Experience in software development and testing, the management or business analyst Experience Scrum
Exam Details: You need to give an answer to a set of 45 questions within 90 minutes. To pass, you must give correct answers to 34 questions.
Career Path
  • Agile Coach
  • Release Tram Engineer
  • Program Consistent
Cost of Certification The first attempt fees are included in the course registration fee itself. However, the condition that the exam should be taken within 30 days after completing the course After that each retake cost $50
Average Salary The average salary of a PMI-ACP is around $123,000 US dollar per year.

Exam details:

  • Exam Duration: 90 minutes
  • Number of questions: 45 (Multiple Choice, Closed Book)
  • Pass Percentage: 75%
  • Requirements: A person should attend an in-person 21-hour training by an IC Agile authorized Training Partner.

Certified Scrum Master

CSM is from Scrum Alliance, which is a private organization based out of USA. This course covers the entire Scrum Framework. Scrum is the leading framework for implementing the Agile methodology, widely benefited software developer all over the world. The organization aim of supporting widespread adoption and effective practice of Agile Scrum.

The Scrum Alliance offers six certification courses for IT and software developers:

  • Certified Scrum Master
  • Certified Scrum Trainer
  • Certified Scrum Developer
  • Certified Scrum Professional
  • Certified Scrum Coach
  • Certified Scrum Product Owner
Certification Advanced Scrum Master certification
Accreditation Body Scrum Alliance
Course Includes It includes interaction, coaching, facilitation, and team dynamics.
Prerequisites
  • Basics of Scrum to understand the framework in a shorter period of time.
  • 1 year of working Experience as a Scrum Master in the Last five years
Career Path
  • Scrum Master
  • Mentor
  • Coach
  • Manager
  • Product Owner
Average salary $100,000 to $ 130,000
Cost of Certification $1295 to $1495
Renewal Cost The certification needs to renew every two years, no extra training or examination.

Exam Details:

  • Exam Duration: 1 hour
  • Number of questions: 50 (Multiple Choice, Closed Book)
  • Pass Percentage: 37 answers should be correct
  • Requirements: You need to attend an in-person, 16-hour course taught by a Certified Scrum Trainer

Certified Scrum Professional

Scrum Alliance provides the Certified Scrum Professional certification. It helps to recognize and demonstrates the ability of the candidate in the advanced skill of artifacts, procedures, roles, and procedures of the Scrum Framework.

Certification Certified Scrum Professional
Accreditation Body Scrum Alliance
Prerequisites

  • An active A-CSM certification
  • 2 years of working Experience as a Scrum Master.

  • It offers a wide range of Agile approaches like FDD, Scrum, Kanban, Crystal, etc.
  • Agile certification helps you to achieve a high rate of growth in their career.
  • Helps professional in discovering new practices for dynamic management of the project. It allows people compare to Agile principles that help you to enhance your teamwork
  • The Agile methodology benefits you to change the mindset and perspective of people working in the industry as project managers
  • Agile certification will earn a lot more money, respect, and honor compared to non-certified professionals.
