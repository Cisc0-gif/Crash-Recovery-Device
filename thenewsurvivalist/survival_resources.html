About the Author

Dr. Stirling Silverman, BA, MS, DC, LAc, chiropractic physician, acupuncturist, herbalist and "Survival Doc" has operated a family-oriented holistic practice for over 30 years and has taught at a major university where he was director of an outpatient teaching clinic. His experience includes living and practicing in Gulfport, Mississippi, where he gained firsthand experience with hurricane evacuation procedures and emergency preparedness. Dr. Silverman is a Christian, has been happily married to the same woman for over 30 years, and is the father of two and grandfather of seven. He has been featured on national television as well as countless radio shows, including the Alex Jones Show. He was a state delegate for Ron Paul, is currently an instructor for the Appleseed Project and the president of the St. Louis/St. Charles Chapter of Oath Keepers.
