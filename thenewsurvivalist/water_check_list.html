Emergency Preparedness Water Check List
    Water "GET" Checklist:
  • [  ] Countertop water filter (e.g., Reverse Osmosis filter)
  • [  ] Portable/backpacker's water filter (e.g., Ceramic filter)
  • [  ] 2.5-5 Gallon (10-20 l) water storage containers (also for transporting)
  • [  ] 55 gallon (200 l) plastic drum
  • [  ] Chlorine bleach (e.g., Clorox)
  • [  ] Calcium hypochlorite (Swimming Pool Shock)
  • [  ] 2% Tincture of iodine
  • [  ] Tarpaulin (for collecting rainwater)
  • [  ] Bottled water (for bug out bags)
  • [  ] Water treatment tablets
