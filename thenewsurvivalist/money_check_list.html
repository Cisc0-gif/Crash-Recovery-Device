    Money "GET" Checklist:

  • [  ] Multi-column ledger book for setting up a family budget (or a budget program for computer)
  • [  ] Safe (or other secure hiding place for home storage of precious metals)
  • [  ] Pocket scale
  • [  ] Magnifying glass or jeweler's loop

    Money "DO" Checklist:
  • [  ] Put emergency cash in emergency stash.
  • [  ] Put emergency cash in bug out bags.
  • [  ] Put emergency cash in car emergency kits.
  • [  ] Visit local coin and bullion dealers.
  • [  ] Check online auctions sites like eBay for precious metals.
  • [  ] Check estate sales for sterling silver and other precious metals.
  • [  ] Set up a written family budget and stick with it.
  • [  ] Pay off all debts and don't use credit anymore (except perhaps for large necessities such as your home.)
  • [  ] Move 20% (or more) of savings and investment funds into precious metals and take physical possession of them.
  • [  ] Keep abreast of the markets by listening to the weekly Financial Sense Newshour at http://www.financialsense.com/.
